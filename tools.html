Quick picks by task

  • Planning a lesson, differentiating a reading, drafting a parent email Google Gemini — general-purpose, approved for staff and students with a guhsd.net account.
  • Giving feedback on student writing, building a quiz from a video or article, generating a rubric Brisk Teaching — staff use only; not currently approved for direct student use.
  • Turning your own uploaded materials into a study guide, grounded only in what you gave it NotebookLM — approved for staff and students with a guhsd.net account.
  • Something not listed below, or a tool you'd like the district to evaluate Use the district's Software & Renewal Requests form — don't use an unreviewed tool with student data in the meantime.

The rules that matter most

  • No student PII in open generative AINever paste student names, grades, IEP details, or other identifying info into a tool marked "No" for PII-secure above.
  • Parent consentStudent work produced in an open generative AI tool requires a signed parent consent form (built into online registration starting 2026–27).
  • Full policyBoard Policy 0441 and Administrative Regulation 0441 are the official word — this page is just a fast reference.
